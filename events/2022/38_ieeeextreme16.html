IEEEXtreme is a global challenge in which teams of IEEE Student members compete in a 24-hour time span to solve a set of programming problems. This Competition is Hosted Simultaneously World-wide.

24 hours (any time can login or logout).

25+ programming languages.

1 or 2 questions added every hour.

No penalty for 'No of submissions done' and time taken for each problem.

30 points are awarded for correct answers. 70 extra points are added depending on the difficulty of task.
